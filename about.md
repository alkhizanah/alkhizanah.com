  <h1>About Al Khizanah</h1>
  <p>Al Khizanah is a group of people gathered to make software. We collaborate on practical tools, shared infrastructure, and open documentation that support reliable product delivery.</p>

    <li>Follow consistent coding styles and write meaningful commit messages</li>
    <li>Review feedback quickly and keep communication respectful</li>
  </ul>
